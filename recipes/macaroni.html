Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A comfortable, homemade meal full of delicious cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 cups Milk
  • 2 1/2 cups Cream
  • 4 cups Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Paprika
